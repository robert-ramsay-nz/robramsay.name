Employment

I'm Rob, a Wellington New Zealand based web-developer and sysadmin who works for domain-name registrar iwantmyname. I’m currently look for work, so if this sounds like someone who'd be a good fit for your team please get in touch.

I have experience in both product-development, contracting, and research environments, and my current role is part of a remote-first team. I love friendly, social workplaces, and the opportunity to learn new things. Over the years I've programmed in a range of languages, with extensive experience in Ruby on Rails, and most recently Elixir and Phoenix. Lately I’ve been focusing on improving my operations and system-administration skills, and I have an interest in UX and design. My preference is for the best language/tool for the job, rather than the newest hotness, and for spaces over tabs.
